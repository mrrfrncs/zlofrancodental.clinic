Extraction

A tooth extraction is a procedure to remove a tooth from the gum socket. It is usually done by a general dentist, an oral surgeon, or a periodontist.

Oral Prophylaxis

Prophylaxis is an important dental treatment for halting the progression of periodontal disease and gingivitis.

Removable Dentures

A removable partial denture or bridge usually consists of replacement teeth attached to a pink or gum-colored plastic base.
